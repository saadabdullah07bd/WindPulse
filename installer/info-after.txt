GETTING STARTED:
----------------
1. Connect your Android device via USB cable.
2. Launch WindPulse from your desktop or Start Menu.
3. Your device should appear automatically — click "Start Mirror".

FOR WIRELESS MIRRORING:
-----------------------
1. First connect via USB and start mirroring once.
2. Click "Switch to Wireless" in the app.
3. Disconnect USB — your device will mirror over Wi-Fi.

WIRELESS PAIRING (No USB needed):
----------------------------------
1. On your phone: Settings > Developer Options > Wireless Debugging
2. Tap "Pair device with pairing code"
3. Enter the IP address and pairing code in WindPulse

TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
- Device not detected? Re-enable USB Debugging and reconnect.
- Wireless not working? Ensure both devices are on the same network.
- Check the Activity Log in the app for detailed error messages.
