Welcome to WindPulse Setup
==========================

WindPulse lets you mirror and control your Android device from your PC
using scrcpy — with a clean, modern interface.

BEFORE YOU INSTALL:
-------------------
1. Make sure USB Debugging is enabled on your Android device.
   (Settings > Developer Options > USB Debugging)

2. For wireless mirroring, ensure your PC and phone are on the same
   Wi-Fi network.

3. Close any running instances of WindPulse before proceeding.

WHAT GETS INSTALLED:
--------------------
- WindPulse application
- Bundled ADB and scrcpy binaries (no separate installation needed)

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
--------------------
- Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- Android 5.0+ device
- USB cable or Wi-Fi connection

Click "Next" to continue with the installation.
